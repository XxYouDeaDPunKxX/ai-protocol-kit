What this is

A practical index for working with AI before the work breaks.

These are not magic prompts. They are working instructions you give to the AI so it knows how to approach a task, what to check, what not to assume, when to stop, and what kind of output is acceptable.

How to use

Give the protocol before the work starts.

  1. Pick the protocol closest to the actual job.
  2. Give it to the AI before execution.
  3. State the task, target files or material, and hard constraints.
  4. Let the protocol guide questions, assumptions, review, and output.

Use the smallest set that fits the task. Do not stack protocols casually.
